Professional Profile

1. Company Overview

Sparsh Tech is a digital-first agency specializing in **web development, mobile apps, AI automation, and cloud-based solutions.** Their core philosophy revolves around simplicity, speed, and smart technology, aiming to deliver **scalable and sustainable** digital products. They handle projects comprehensively, from strategy and conceptualization to building and post-launch maintenance.

2. Services Provided

  • Web Development: Highly responsive websites, landing pages, and web applications.
  • Mobile Applications: Native and cross-platform app development.
  • AI & Automation: Chatbots, workflow automation, and intelligent systems (LLM/API based).
  • Cloud Solutions: Migration, serverless setups, and security.
  • UI / UX Design: User-centric designs, wireframes, and prototypes.
  • Custom Software: Bespoke business tools (CRM, ERP, internal dashboards).

3. Technology Stack

  • Frontend: React.js, Next.js, Tailwind CSS (for UI design).
  • Backend & Database: Node.js + Express.js, MongoDB.
  • Automation: N8N workflows, APIs (Gmail, LinkedIn, WhatsApp), and LLMs (OpenAI/Gemini).
  • Architecture: Designed for fast-loading, SEO-optimized, and highly interactive products.

4. Vision & Values

  • Global Reach: Creating technology solutions for clients worldwide.
  • Accessibility: Making advanced digital solutions affordable for small/medium businesses.
  • Sustainability: Building future-proof technology that offers long-term value.
  • Client-Centric: Maintaining close collaboration from discovery to post-launch.

5. Process & Delivery Methodology

  1. Discovery: Understand goals, pain-points, and requirements.
  2. Design: Wireframes, prototypes, and UX design to validate ideas visually.
  3. Development: Build using modern, scalable web and backend technologies.
  4. Testing: Rigorous QA for performance, usability, and security.
  5. Deployment: Smooth product launch to production.
  6. Support & Maintenance: Ongoing improvements, bug fixes, and performance optimization.
